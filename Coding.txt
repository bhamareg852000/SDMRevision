SDM (Software Development Methodologies) is a subject commonly taught in software engineering, computer science, and related courses, including CDAC. It focuses on the processes, principles, and best practices for developing software in a structured and efficient way.

Here's an overview of SDM:

1. What is SDM?
Software Development Methodologies are frameworks or processes used to structure, plan, and control the development of software systems. They define the steps, tools, and roles required to deliver high-quality software.
